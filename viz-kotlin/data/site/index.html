Scoring FAQ

  • Your daily time to complete each two-part puzzle (worth 2 stars) is measured as "time since the puzzle unlocked at midnight Eastern".

  • Last year, 13 people earned every star. Ties are broken by the lowest median daily time (in other words, the average of your 6th and 7th fastest daily time out of 12 if you finish every puzzle).
